M&TT Colloquia 2022

Instructions for presenters

Before your presentation
Hand in 3 weeks before the presentation (using the Data Form from below):

  • Short title (max. 40 characters)
  • Long title (can be the same as the short title)
  • Abstract (min. 100 words), preferably
    • Gives a motivation behind the topic
    • Describes content of the presentation
    • Challenges the reader to raise questions/topics for discussion
  • A picture (max 1MB)
  • Your department and office number

The presentation itself

  • Is max. 15 minutes (approx. 10 slides)
  • The presentation should contain in-depth explanation of your recent research focusing on relevance, ideas and novelty. It could also be about something else than your research, but covers (an opinion on) a topic related to academia (e.g. open science, diversity)
  • The content should encourage people to ask questions and to start a discussion,
  • No detailed description of results, derivation of analytic relations or basic knowledge derived from Bachelors.
